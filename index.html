Megan's Nerdy Finance Stuff

Hello New Friends!

So why did I decide to use my CS50 assignment to make a webpage about personal finance?

The year I was born my mother put in $3,000 into a investment account for me that she decided not to touch until I turned 18. Once I turned 18, she turned this account over to me and I learned that over time that $3,000 had grown to nearly $12,000. There was no day trading involved, just a one time investment into an index fund that grew and compounded over time.
Since then, I have become very interested in the personal finance space and have become rather nerdy about the topic along the way. As I learned more, I wanted to help others gain the personal finance education that I was so lucky to have received from my family. This has led to me talking often about the topic to anyone that will listen. I have also been known to spend whole days researching different tax advantaged investment accounts across Canada, the UK and France to help educate international friends.
This site is an expansion of that interest and one day, I might like to build it out further to create more educational resources. Until then, feel free to use this site to learn more about finance terms, play with a compound interest calculator, or view my recommendations for personal finance educators that I have learned from.
